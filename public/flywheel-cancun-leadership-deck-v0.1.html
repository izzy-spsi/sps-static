The Pivot

Then September 11 happened.

I was working with United Airlines in Chicago. Overnight, everything changed. I had to pivot — and somehow, I ended up in taxis.

I had never even taken a taxi before.

But I saw something nobody else was paying attention to. Taxi drivers were working hard — harder than almost anyone — and they were being left behind by technology. No credit cards. No digital meter. No data. No way to compete.

We didn't follow the trend.
We built it.
