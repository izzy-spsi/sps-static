Our Story

More Than a Cafe.
An Experience.

Domo Cafe is NHK's beloved character come to life — a Japanese-inspired cafe where playful design meets bold, craveable food.

Every corner is crafted to surprise and delight. From the moment you walk in, you're inside the world of Domo: warm, a little wild, and completely unforgettable.

We're obsessed with hospitality with heart — the kind that makes you feel seen, welcomed, and hungry to come back.

Based in Buena Park, California, we're proud to call this community home and even prouder to open our doors here first.
