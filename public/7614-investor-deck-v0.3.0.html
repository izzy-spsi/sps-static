Entitlement Strategy
The Special
Permit Path
7614 requires a Special Permit under ZR 74-95 to achieve its full 13-story, 141' massing. Two zoning variances require City Planning approval — both are standard and well-precedented for mixed-income developments of this type.
Variance 1 — Building Height
As-of-right maximum height 95'
Base height proposed 57' ✓ Complies
Full building height proposed 141' · ZR 74-95
Variance 2 — Open Area
Min. distance between buildings 40' required
Proposed distance to church 4.5' · ZR 74-95
Why ZR 74-95 Works Here
R7B with qualifying affordable housing (25%) is a strong basis for City Planning approval — height bonuses for affordability are core to NYC housing policy
All other zoning metrics comply — lot coverage (68.8% vs 100% max), setbacks (7' provided), FAR (62,018 vs 62,260 max)
Fontan Architecture has a track record of navigating Special Permits in NYC's outer boroughs — DOB filing active as of January 2026
