What's Next

Four things to move
in the next 30 days.
01
Reduce offers-per-ride
Every completed Uber 3P trip requires 34.83 offers. Improving driver engagement, offer targeting, and response rates is the highest-leverage dispatch improvement available. A 20% reduction in offers-per-ride would meaningfully increase throughput without adding drivers.
02
Unlock Las Vegas
Las Vegas received 575K offers — the second-largest market by volume — but has only a 2.0% acceptance rate vs 6–7% elsewhere. It operates through a 3P supply partner. Understanding why and improving driver-offer fit here is a major growth unlock.
03
Activate Houston
Houston has 5 drivers, 8 completed rides — live but pre-scale. The market activation playbook from San Diego and Palm Springs should apply here. Set a 90-day target: 30+ active drivers, 1,000+ monthly trips.
04
Shift 1P to digital payments
74.7% of Flywheel 1P trips are cash. Card and QR code trips average $51.99 and $60.66 respectively vs $40.96 for cash — and digital payments unlock data, retention tools, and Uber integration pathways. Sacramento is already at 71.8% digital. That's the model to replicate.
