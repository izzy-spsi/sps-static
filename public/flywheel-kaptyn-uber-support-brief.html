The Support Process — Backend Only, No Flywheel Hardware
1

Issue Reported by Kaptyn

Kaptyn operator flags a driver not receiving Uber offers through their dispatch system. They contact Flywheel — one call, one point of contact. No direct Uber interaction needed on Kaptyn's side.

Kaptyn → Flywheel
2

Driver Record Validation — Flywheel Fleets

Flywheel checks the Fleets portal — all driver info correct, license not expired, phone verified, Uber checkmarks active under Driver › Edit. Entirely backend. Driver doesn't touch anything.

Flywheel Backend
3

Vehicle Record Validation — Flywheel Fleets

Confirm make, model, color, license plate are accurate. Uber checkmarks active under Vehicle › Edit. Any mismatch blocks Uber offer eligibility at the integration layer.

Flywheel Backend
4

Uber Supplier Portal Verification

supplier.uber.com — correct market selected, driver status Active, vehicle status Active. This is Flywheel's view into Uber's system. Kaptyn doesn't have this portal access — Flywheel does.

Uber Supplier Portal · Flywheel Access Only
5

Identify Issue Type + Ban Check

Cross-reference Flywheel's internal ban list. If flagged: Flywheel communicates to Kaptyn, who relays to driver. Driver appeals directly to Uber. Flywheel monitors the appeal and manages re-onboarding if overturned.

Resolution Path — Driver Appeal to Uber
6

Flywheel Backend Review

If not banned — Flywheel dev team reviews via internal Slack (market_lasvegas channel). Driver name, phone, cab number, issue description. Kaptyn gets a status update — not a ticket to manage themselves.

Flywheel Dev Team · Internal
7

Uber External Escalation

If backend is clean — Flywheel escalates directly to ubertaxisupport@uber.com. Flywheel owns the thread: market/area, driver name, phone, email, cab number, full issue description. Kaptyn is never in the Uber Support loop — that's Flywheel's lane.

Uber Direct · 24hr SLA
8

Confirmed Live — Kaptyn Notified

Flywheel confirms driver is live and receiving Uber offers through Kaptyn's dispatch. Clean close-out communicated to Kaptyn. Driver workflow never changed — it just works now.
