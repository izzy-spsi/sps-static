📝 Full Text — Memorize This

Welcome to Domo Cafe.

I want to start by saying — thank you. Genuinely. Not the kind of thank you you say because it's polite. The kind you say because it means something.

You could be anywhere right now. And you're here.

That tells me you already get it — that this is more than a lunch, more than a media moment, more than a business meeting. You showed up because something about this felt worth showing up for.

You were right.

In this room right now, we have three groups of people who each play a different role in what happens next. We have influencers — the people who shape culture. We have media — the people who tell the story. And we have partners — the people who help build what comes after today.

All three of you matter. All three of you are part of this.

So settle in. Eat something. And let me tell you what we built here — and where we're going.

[Pause. Let them feel it. Then move into Part 2.]
