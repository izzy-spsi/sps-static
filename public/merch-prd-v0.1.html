01

Overview

Vision

Build a unified merchandise management portal that enables Domo Cafe to design, produce, inventory, sell, and report on both house-branded and NHK-licensed merchandise — while maintaining full compliance with licensing agreements and streamlining the approval-to-shelf workflow.

Problem Statement

Domo Cafe operates two distinct merchandise tracks — house-branded products and NHK/Domo-kun licensed items — each with fundamentally different compliance, approval, and royalty requirements. Without a centralized system:

  • Design approvals are managed via email, creating audit gaps
  • Royalty calculations are manual and error-prone
  • Inventory visibility is siloed between POS and vendor systems
  • There is no single source of truth for product catalog, SKUs, or sales data
  • NHK reporting is time-consuming and inconsistent
Scope
✅ In Scope
  • House merch catalog management
  • NHK-licensed product management
  • Design approval workflow
  • Inventory tracking
  • Sales & royalty reporting
  • Vendor management (L2P + others)
  • Toast POS integration
  • Shopify / storefront integration
❌ Out of Scope (v0.1)
  • Full ERP / accounting integration
  • Manufacturing management
  • International shipping logistics
  • Consumer-facing storefront (integration only)
  • Brand licensing for non-NHK IP
Key Metrics
~30% Reduction in approval cycle time (target)
100% NHK royalty accuracy (zero manual errors)
[ TBD ] SKU count at launch

Portal Features

Product Catalog
  • Multi-variant product management (size, color, material, SKU)
  • Product type flags: house / licensed / internal
  • Variant matrix builder (auto-generate SKUs)
  • Product image gallery (mockup + production photos)
  • Linked approval records (for licensed items)
  • Cost + retail price fields with margin calculator
  • Barcode / QR code generation per SKU
Vendor Management
  • Vendor profiles (L2P + future vendors)
  • Product ↔ vendor mapping
  • PO generation and tracking
  • Lead time tracking per vendor
  • Vendor performance history
  • Contact directory per vendor
Design Approval Workflow (NHK)
  • Upload mockup + spec sheets per product
  • Submission tracker (submitted / under review / approved / rejected)
  • NHK approval reference number field
  • Revision history per submission
  • Email notification on status change
  • Rejection reason capture + design revision request
  • Approval expiry tracking [ TBD: NHK approval validity window ]
Inventory Tracker
  • Real-time stock levels per SKU per location
  • Par levels with automated reorder alerts
  • Inventory receipt log (date, quantity, PO reference)
  • Shrinkage / adjustment tracking
  • Low-stock dashboard widget
  • Multi-location support (cafe floor, stockroom, online) [ TBD ]
Sales Dashboard
  • Units sold by channel (DTC in-store, DTC online, wholesale)
  • Revenue by product / category / channel
  • Royalties owed (auto-calculated) by period
  • Best sellers + slow movers
  • Date range filtering (daily / weekly / monthly / custom)
  • Export to CSV / PDF
Royalty Calculator
  • Auto-applies 8% (DTC) or 13% (wholesale) on net sales
  • 2% marketing fee auto-applied on net wholesale
  • MG tracker: shows YTD royalties vs. $35,000 MG
  • Monthly NHK statement generator (PDF)
  • Overage / underage flagging
  • Audit trail for all calculations
Order Management
  • In-store orders via Toast POS (synced)
  • Online orders via Shopify or custom storefront (synced)
  • Wholesale order entry + tracking
  • Fulfillment status per order
  • Returns + exchanges processing
  • Order history searchable by SKU, customer, date
