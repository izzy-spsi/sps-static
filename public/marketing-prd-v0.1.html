01

Overview

Vision

Build a unified marketing operations portal that gives Domo Cafe (and the broader venture portfolio) a single command center to create, schedule, publish, and analyze all marketing activity — from social posts to grand opening campaigns — powered by Jarvis AI assistance and integrated with every customer touchpoint.

Problem Statement

Across Domo Cafe and the venture portfolio, marketing is fragmented:

  • Campaigns are managed across Google Docs, group chats, and individual social apps
  • No single content calendar exists — drops are ad-hoc or missed
  • Brand assets are scattered — no enforced guidelines, off-brand content ships
  • Analytics are siloed: IG, TikTok, email, and in-store data never meet
  • The influencer pipeline has no tracking — deliverables fall through the cracks
  • Campaign ROI is impossible to measure without a unified view
  • Jarvis AI capabilities are underused for copy, captions, and strategy
Scope
✅ In Scope
  • Campaign lifecycle management
  • Content calendar and scheduling
  • Brand asset library
  • Multi-channel publishing
  • Analytics dashboard
  • Influencer tracking
  • Budget management per campaign
  • AI copy assistant (Jarvis)
  • Loyalty/Chomps tie-in
❌ Out of Scope (v0.1)
  • Full CRM / customer data platform
  • Paid ad management (Meta Ads, Google Ads)
  • PR wire distribution
  • Video editing / production tools
  • E-commerce cart / checkout
  • Real-time foot traffic data

05

Portal Features

📋
Campaign Manager
Create, track, and close campaigns with brief, timeline, budget, and KPIs. Status: Draft / Active / Paused / Completed.
📅
Content Calendar
Drag-drop scheduling across all channels. 30/60/90-day views. Color-coded by venture and content type.
🗂️
Asset Library
Upload, tag, and search brand assets. Venture-separated. NHK-approved art in locked library with license metadata.
✍️
Post Composer
Multi-channel publish from one interface. Character counters, hashtag suggestions, media upload. Schedule or publish now.
📊
Analytics Dashboard
Reach, engagement, conversions, foot traffic correlation. Per-campaign and per-channel views. Export to PDF.
🤳
Influencer Tracker
Contact directory, campaign assignments, deliverable tracking, payment status, performance data per creator.
💰
Budget Tracker
Per-campaign spend vs. budget. Actuals vs. planned. Alert when budget threshold exceeded.
🤖
AI Copy Assistant (Jarvis)
Draft captions, email copy, press releases, SMS messages. Tone-aware per venture. Editable before publish.
💛
Loyalty / Chomps Tie-In
Campaign triggers based on Chomps loyalty activity. Target by tier, visit count, spend, or inactivity.
✅
Approval Workflow
Draft → Review → Approved → Scheduled → Published. Brand compliance check for NHK content. Comment threads per asset.
