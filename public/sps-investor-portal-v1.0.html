Phase 1 — Idea to Investor: 8 AI Agents

Unlike traditional startup programs that begin with a business idea, SPS begins with the founder. The platform asks one question: “Who are you, and what problem are you uniquely qualified to solve?” From there, eight AI-powered agents guide the founder all the way to a funded, validated venture.

01
Ideation Agent FOUNDER DISCOVERY
Uncovers founder background, skills, motivations, and founder-market fit. Scores the match between who you are and what you want to build. Output: Founder Venture Brief
02
Market Research Agent INTELLIGENCE
Researches industry trends, competitors, customer segments, and risks. Powered by GeoPulse (location intelligence) and VIS (AI-generated focus groups). Output: Market Intelligence Report
03
TAM / SAM / SOM Agent MARKET SIZING
Calculates Total, Serviceable, and Obtainable market. Grounds the opportunity in real numbers. Output: Market Opportunity Model
04
Business Model Agent REVENUE DESIGN
Determines revenue streams, pricing, customer acquisition, and path to profitability. Output: Business Blueprint
05
Cost & Asset Agent ECONOMICS
Determines startup costs, capital requirements, asset classification (light/medium/heavy), and staffing needs. Output: Launch Economics Report
06
Validation Agent PROOF OF DEMAND
Creates customer interview plans, landing page concepts, MVP frameworks, and real-world validation tests. Output: Validation Roadmap
07
Investor Readiness Agent RAISE PREP
Prepares financial assumptions, fundraising strategy, and investor materials tailored to the venture's stage and market. Output: Investor Package
08
Pitch Deck Agent THE ROOM
Generates a complete pitch deck, executive summary, and investor narrative. Everything a founder needs to walk into a room and raise capital. Output: Investor-Ready Pitch Deck
