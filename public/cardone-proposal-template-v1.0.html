Program Overview

CardONE is CabConnect's debit card–based paratransit ride credit system, purpose-built for municipal transit agencies and paratransit programs. It replaces paper coupons, vouchers, and manual reimbursement workflows with a streamlined, auditable digital payment rail.

With CardONE, your agency issues pre-loaded debit cards to eligible riders. Cards are redeemed at the point of service — no paper changing hands, no manual reconciliation at month-end. Every trip is logged in real time, giving program administrators full visibility into utilization, spend, and rider activity from a single dashboard.

CardONE is designed to integrate with any licensed taxi or rideshare provider, making it the most flexible ride credit tool available for paratransit programs of any size.

2

How It Works

1

Rider Enrolls

Eligible riders apply through your agency's enrollment process. Optional: use the CardONE Online Intake Portal for fully digital, paperless enrollment with e-signature support.

2

Card Issued

CabConnect produces and ships branded or co-branded debit cards (minimum 500-card production run). Cards are loaded with the municipality-defined credit balance before mailing.

3

Rider Books a Ride

Riders contact their preferred participating taxi or rideshare provider and present their CardONE card as the payment method. Optional: riders can view trip history and manage their account via the Rider Self-Service Portal.

4

Driver Charges the Card

The provider processes the fare directly against the rider's CardONE balance at the point of service — no vouchers, no cash, no manual paperwork. The transaction is recorded in real time.

5

Municipality Reviews & Reimburses

Your program administrator accesses the CardONE dashboard to review all trip activity, export reports, and manage reimbursement. Full audit trail available at any time.

3

Program Features

Real-Time Card Balance

Card balances update instantly at point of sale. Administrators and riders always see accurate, up-to-date credit availability.

Trip Reporting & Audit Trail

Every transaction is logged with date, time, provider, and fare amount. Export reports in CSV or PDF for grant compliance and internal audits.

500-Card Minimum Production Run

Cards are produced and shipped in batches. The minimum order is 500 cards, giving programs flexibility to right-size initial inventory.

Provider Agnostic

Works with any licensed taxi or rideshare provider. No exclusive contracts — your riders use who they trust.

Online Intake / Sign-Up Portal Add-On

Fully digital enrollment flow with e-signature. Eliminates paper applications and reduces processing time. $25/mo.

Rider Self-Service Portal Add-On

Riders log in to view ride history, check balances, and manage their account. Reduces inbound calls to your office. $25/mo.
