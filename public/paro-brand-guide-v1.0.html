05 · Voice & Tone
How Paro Speaks

Five dimensions. Every word earns its place.

Dimension Principle ✓ Do Say ✗ Don't Say
Clinical
not cold		 Lead with validated data. Numbers first. Specificity is warmth. "7 validated inflammatory biomarkers" "may help with inflammation"
Warm
not fluffy		 Connect data to human benefit. Never sacrifice specificity for feeling. "Your body deserves proof." "Feel amazing every day!"
Considered
not over-explained		 Lead with the claim. Support with science. Stop. No throat-clearing. "100mg. Phase 1 validated. No compromises." "We believe that wellness should be backed by science, and that's why we partnered with..."
Honest
not hype		 Specific claims backed by data. Never exaggerate beyond the evidence. "30–50× more bioavailable than commodity ginger" "Revolutionary breakthrough formula"
Premium
not exclusive		 Approachable excellence. Rigor without arrogance. Science for everyone. "The only ginger shot backed by human clinical data." "For those who demand only the best."
Claim Language Guidelines

✓ Backed by Phase 1 Data — You Can Say

  • → "7 validated inflammatory biomarkers"
  • → "50-participant Phase 1 clinical trial"
  • → "0 adverse events reported"
  • → "30–50× more bioavailable than commodity ginger"
  • → "100mg standardized active phenolics per shot"
  • → "The only ginger extract with Phase 1 human clinical data"

✗ Requires FDA/FTC Caution — Do Not Say

  • → "Treats inflammation" (disease claim)
  • → "Cures joint pain" (disease claim)
  • → "Clinically proven" without disclaimer
  • → Any claim implying Phase 3 drug approval
  • → "FDA approved" (supplements are not drugs)
  • → Guaranteed results on health outcomes
Required Disclaimer (Phase 1 References)

*These statements have not been evaluated by the Food and Drug Administration. This product is not intended to diagnose, treat, cure, or prevent any disease. Clinical data references Evithé Biotechnology's Phase 1 trial (n=50). Results may vary.

06 · Product Presentation
Visual Language

The amber glass vial is always the hero. Always.

Paro Daily Shot · 2oz Amber Glass · Always the Hero

✓ Shot Photography DOs

  • Natural light — golden hour, warm tones
  • Earthy surfaces: wood, stone, linen, marble
  • Botanical props: ginger root, herbs, leaves
  • Morning ritual setting — calm, intentional
  • Amber glass vial always visible and prominent
  • Warm color temperature throughout
  • Active wellness: yoga mat, morning coffee adjacent
  • Real hands, real settings — no sterility

✗ Photography DON'Ts

  • Stock photo health imagery of any kind
  • Fake doctors, lab coats, clinical white settings
  • Before/after photography
  • Gym, CrossFit, heavy fitness contexts
  • Cool/blue color temperature
  • Overly retouched, perfected imagery
  • Pharmaceutical visual language
  • Plastic packaging visible
Color Temperature & Mood
✓ WARM GOLDEN

Color temp 2700–4000K. Think golden hour. Warm shadows. Amber tones dominant.

✗ COOL CLINICAL

Never. Cool blues and grays read pharmaceutical, not wellness ritual.

07 · Digital Guidelines
Digital Standards

Consistent across every screen, every format.

Web

  • Dark mode default (Espresso background)
  • Light mode toggle available top-right
  • Terracotta as primary CTA color
  • Minimum 16px body text on desktop
  • Max content width: 1200px
  • Navigation: sticky, translucent on scroll

Email

  • Light background (Linen) always
  • Espresso header band with Linen logo
  • Max width: 600px
  • Terracotta CTA buttons only
  • Plain text fallback available
  • Preheader text: always present

Social Media

  • Square format (1080×1080) primary
  • Terracotta as social accent color
  • PARO wordmark always present
  • Minimal text overlay — visuals lead
  • Amber vial in 80%+ of product posts
  • JetBrains Mono for data callouts

Accessibility

  • WCAG 2.1 AA minimum contrast ratio
  • Espresso on Linen: 12.6:1 ✓
  • Terracotta on Linen: 4.8:1 ✓
  • Linen on Espresso: 12.6:1 ✓
  • Alt text on all product images
  • Keyboard navigation on all web assets
Spacing System — 8px Grid
8px Grid — all spacing values are multiples of 8.
8 · 16 · 24 · 32 · 40 · 48 · 64px
Minimum padding: 16px on all sides. Generous section padding: 64px+.
Button Standards
  • Primary: Terracotta fill, Linen text — subscription/purchase actions

  • Tertiary: Honey fill, Espresso text — supporting CTAs
