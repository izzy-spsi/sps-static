The Opportunity

Paro enters a $410M wellness shots market dominated by PE-backed brands with zero clinical science. Top 2 players — Suja ($327M) and So Good So You ($100M+) — control 73% of the market. Both are science-free.

Paro's hero ingredient is Evithé's patented, Phase 1-validated ginger extract: the only ginger extract in the world with human clinical trial data (50 participants, 7 inflammatory biomarkers, 0 adverse events, 3 patents). 100mg active phenolics vs. 5–15mg commodity. 30–50× more bioavailable.

Revenue Architecture

Core: DTC subscription (shot + capsules) — predictable recurring revenue with premium clinical pricing.

Phase 2: Erewhon exclusive (M4) → Whole Foods / Sprouts (M8) → Mass retail (Yr 2+)
