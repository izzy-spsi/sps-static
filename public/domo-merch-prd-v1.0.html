01
Executive Summary
What We're Building

The Domo Cafe Merchandise Portal is a centralized operations platform for managing the full lifecycle of Domo Cafe merchandise — from initial design brief through NHK approval, vendor production, inventory receipt, and royalty reporting.

We operate two distinct merchandise tracks: House Merch (GFC BP LLC-branded items requiring no character licensing) and Licensed Merch (items featuring the Domo-kun character, requiring written NHK approval via Striker Entertainment before any production begins).

⚠️ NHK Compliance

Any merchandise featuring the Domo-kun character must receive written approval from Striker Entertainment before going to production. Unauthorized production constitutes a breach of the NHK Merchandising License Agreement (executed May 19, 2026) and may result in license termination.

🏷️
House Merch
GFC BP LLC branded. Cups, totes, tees, aprons, hats, stickers. No NHK approval needed. Faster to market.
⭐
Licensed Merch
Domo-kun character items. Pins, plushies, collectibles, character mugs. Full NHK approval workflow required.
📌
Pin Series
Domo Cafe Crew — monthly limited edition pins. Domo in cafe roles. Physical pin = digital collectible twin.

Success in 12 months: Zero unauthorized NHK submissions reach production. 100% royalty reporting accuracy. Less than 14-day vendor-to-shelf lead time for reorders. Full sell-through visibility per SKU.

02
Problem Statement
Where We Are Today

Currently, Domo Cafe's merchandise operations are entirely manual and ad hoc. There is no central system tracking designs in progress, NHK approval status, vendor relationships, purchase orders, or inventory levels.

No Approval Tracking
Designs submitted to Striker Entertainment via email with no status visibility. Risk of production beginning before written approval is received.
No Inventory Visibility
No system to track what's in stockroom vs. display floor vs. sold. Reorders happen reactively when items run out.
No Royalty Tracking
NHK requires quarterly royalty reports. With no sales-by-SKU tracking, calculating 8% DTC and 13% wholesale royalties manually is error-prone.
Vendor Fragmentation
Multiple vendors, no central directory. Lead times, MOQs, and pricing scattered across emails. No PO trail.
⚠️ Compliance Risk

The NHK Merchandising License Agreement requires all Domo-kun merchandise to be approved in writing before production. Without a tracking system, there is a real risk that unapproved items enter production — a potential breach that could jeopardize the license and the 25-mile exclusivity radius for Buena Park.

Minimum Guarantee

The NHK MLA carries a $35,000 minimum guarantee for the 12-month term. $10,000 advance was paid at signing. Remaining $25,000 is recoupable against earned royalties. Breakeven on MG = $1,167,000 in DTC F&B sales at 3%, or $437,500 in merch DTC sales at 8%. The portal must surface MG recoupment progress in the royalty dashboard.

Quarterly Royalty Report

System generates a quarterly report showing: sales by SKU, channel breakdown, gross royalty owed, MG credit applied, net royalty due. Exportable as PDF for submission to NHK/Striker.

⚠️ NHK Approval Required Per Design

Each pin design must be individually submitted to Striker Entertainment and receive written NHK approval before production. Approval must be logged in the portal against the specific SKU. No exceptions.

Physical ↔ Digital Twin

Each physical pin sold triggers a digital collectible mint in the Domo Loyalty App. The portal manages the pairing: when a pin SKU is marked "sold," it fires an API call to the loyalty system to mint the corresponding digital collectible for that customer's account.

Staff Pin Progression

Staff members earn the corresponding pin when they are certified in that role. The portal tracks which roles each staff member has unlocked and their remaining progression. This is separate from the retail pin drop — staff copies are non-sale items tracked as staff benefits.

Technical Architecture
How It's Built
Frontend
Next.js 14 + TypeScript. React Server Components for data-heavy views. Tailwind CSS. Hosted on Railway.
Database
PostgreSQL on Railway. Prisma ORM. Separate tables for SKUs, designs, approvals, POs, inventory, royalties.
Auth
Clerk or NextAuth. Role-based: Operator, Brand Manager, Licensing Coordinator, Vendor, Staff.
File Storage
Cloudflare R2 or AWS S3. Design files, mockups, NHK submission packages, PO PDFs.
Email
Resend API. Approval notifications to Striker, PO emails to vendors, low-stock alerts, royalty report delivery.
Jarvis API
GET /api/jarvis/status + full CRUD. Auth: x-api-key header = JARVIS_API_KEY env var. Required on all builds.
