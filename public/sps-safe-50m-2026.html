3. Definitions

"Pre-Money Valuation Cap" $50,000,000 (fifty million U.S. dollars), being the pre-investment Company valuation used to calculate the Conversion Price. Post-money valuation (assuming full $5M raise) = approximately $55,000,000. Investor's approximate ownership at conversion: Purchase Amount ÷ $55,000,000.
"Discount Rate" 80% (i.e., Investor receives a 20% discount to the price per share in an Equity Financing).
"Equity Financing" A bona fide transaction or series of transactions with the principal purpose of raising capital, pursuant to which the Company issues and sells Preferred Stock at a fixed pre-money valuation, with aggregate gross proceeds to the Company of not less than $1,000,000 (including the conversion of this SAFE and all other SAFEs).
"Conversion Price" The lesser of: (i) the Cap Price (the price per share derived from the $50,000,000 pre-money valuation cap ÷ Company Capitalization immediately prior to the Equity Financing, on a fully diluted basis), and (ii) the Discount Price (price per share of Standard Preferred Stock sold in the Equity Financing × Discount Rate of 80%).
"SAFE Preferred Stock" A series of Preferred Stock issued to Investor upon conversion of this SAFE, with the same rights, privileges, preferences, and restrictions as the Standard Preferred Stock sold in the Equity Financing, except as to price.
"Liquidity Event" A Change of Control (acquisition, merger, or sale of substantially all assets) or an IPO.
"Dissolution Event" A voluntary termination of operations, a general assignment for the benefit of Company's creditors, or any other liquidation or dissolution of the Company.
"Pro-Rata Rights Agreement" A written agreement between the Company and Investor providing Investor the right to purchase Investor's pro-rata share of securities in future equity financings.

4. Conversion Events

4.1 Equity Financing. Upon the Company's next Equity Financing (a "Qualified Financing"), this SAFE will automatically convert into SAFE Preferred Stock. The number of shares issued to Investor shall equal the Purchase Amount divided by the Conversion Price.

4.2 Liquidity Event (before Equity Financing). If a Liquidity Event occurs before conversion in an Equity Financing, the Investor shall receive either: (i) a cash payment equal to the Purchase Amount, or (ii) at the Investor's election, shares of Common Stock equal to the Purchase Amount divided by the Liquidity Price (lesser of Cap Price or Discount Price based on Liquidity Event valuation), whichever is greater.

4.3 Dissolution Event. If a Dissolution Event occurs, the Company shall pay Investor the Purchase Amount (to the extent legally permitted) before any distributions to common stockholders.

5. Most Favored Nation (MFN)

If the Company issues any SAFEs or convertible instruments to other investors after the Effective Date of this SAFE, and any such subsequent instrument includes terms more favorable to such investor than those set forth in this SAFE (e.g., a lower Valuation Cap, a higher Discount Rate, or additional rights), the Company shall promptly notify Investor, and Investor shall have the right — at its sole election, within thirty (30) calendar days of such notice — to amend this SAFE to include such more favorable terms.

6. Pro-Rata Rights
