Vision
Not a SaaS signup.
An initiation.
"Joining SPS shouldn't feel like signing up for a tool.
It should feel like being accepted into something."
— Product Principle

The SPS Membership Platform is a full web app that takes a founder from first visit to active member with a dedicated AI Regent. The Ideation Agent is a mandatory first step — not optional. The chatbot grows with the member through all 10 stages of the SPS founder journey.

Requirements · Intake Chatbot
A locked, constrained conversation.

The intake chatbot collects member profile information through natural conversation. It is not a general AI — it has one job. It cannot be manipulated into going off-topic.

REQ-008
Constrained Scope
Chatbot only performs two functions: (1) collect intake profile info and (2) answer questions about SPS membership tiers and benefits. Any other topic is redirected.
Proposed
REQ-009
Required Fields Collection
Chatbot must collect: Full Name, Email, Phone (optional), LinkedIn URL, Current Role, Industry, Location, and a brief statement of what they're working on or thinking about building.
Proposed
REQ-010
Off-Topic Redirect
If user asks anything outside membership scope, chatbot responds: "I'm here to help you get started with SPS membership. Let me pick up where we left off..." and resumes intake.
Proposed
REQ-011
Membership FAQ Awareness
Chatbot knows all 5 membership tiers, pricing, founding discount eligibility, spot limits, and the $2 deposit model. Can answer any question about membership.
Proposed
REQ-012
Graceful Completion
When all required fields are collected, chatbot confirms the information, thanks the member, and transitions them to email verification.
Proposed
REQ-013
Session Persistence
Intake session is saved continuously. If user closes the browser and returns, they resume exactly where they left off.
Proposed

Requirements · A.I.R. — AI Regent
The north star.

Every SPS member eventually gets their own dedicated AI Regent — a personal AI that knows their entire story, hosted and maintained by SPS.

PHASE 1 · NOW
Shared Chatbot
Stage-aware, context-persistent chatbot guides all members through the 10-stage journey. Shared infrastructure, personalized context per member.
PHASE 2 · FUTURE
Dedicated A.I.R.
Each member gets their own dedicated AI Regent — named by the member. Powered by OpenClaw/Hermes. Hosted and maintained by SPS. Full conversation history from Phase 1 transfers seamlessly.
REQ-038
Phase 1 — Shared Stage-Aware Chatbot
The persistent chatbot described in REQ-014–018 serves as the Phase 1 A.I.R. for all members. Must be architected so conversation history can transfer to a dedicated instance in Phase 2.
Proposed
REQ-039
Phase 2 — Dedicated Instance Architecture
System must be designed to provision a dedicated OpenClaw/Hermes instance per member. Each instance is isolated, persistent, and scoped to that member's data only.
Proposed
REQ-040
History Portability
When a member graduates to a dedicated A.I.R., their full conversation history, brief versions, scores, and stage data migrate with zero data loss. Member names their A.I.R. at activation.
