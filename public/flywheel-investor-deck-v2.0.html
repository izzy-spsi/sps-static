03 — Problem

The Industry Is Broken

The $36B taxi industry has been left behind — fragmented, hardware-locked, and shut out of modern ride demand.

01
Taxi drivers can't access Uber demand

Until Flywheel, taxis had zero access to Uber's 12M daily trips — the largest demand channel in the world.

02
Complex hardware integration required

Fleet operators must navigate fragmented meter, dispatch, and tablet systems with no unified solution.

03
Individual drivers locked out

All competitor solutions are fleet-only. The 5–10M independent licensed taxi drivers globally have no path in.

04
No earnings transparency or driver tools

Drivers operate blind — no real-time incentive data, no earnings analytics, no upfront pricing.

05
Fragmented dispatch systems

Each city runs a different dispatch platform. No operator has built a cross-city taxi OS.

06
No unified platform OS for taxi operations

The industry needs a single operating system — one platform across payments, dispatch, Uber, incentives, and compliance.
