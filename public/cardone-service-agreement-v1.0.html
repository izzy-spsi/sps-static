§ 1Definitions
"CardONE System"
Means CabConnect's proprietary software platform, including all web-based portals, APIs, databases, card issuance infrastructure, transaction processing services, and related documentation made available to Licensee under this Agreement, as updated or modified by CabConnect from time to time.
"Authorized Users"
Means Licensee's employees, contractors, and call takers who are authorized by Licensee to access and use the CardONE System on behalf of Licensee in accordance with this Agreement.
"Riders"
Means the individual paratransit program beneficiaries enrolled by Licensee in its ride credit program who receive and use Cards issued through the CardONE System.
"Transaction"
Means each discrete debit card authorization, redemption, or fund transfer event processed through the CardONE System on behalf of a Rider, including but not limited to ride credits, adjustments, and refunds.
"Card"
Means each physical or virtual prepaid/debit card produced and managed by CabConnect through the CardONE System for distribution to Riders as part of Licensee's paratransit ride credit program.
"Admin Portal"
Means the web-based administrative interface within the CardONE System accessible to Authorized Users for managing Rider accounts, loading ride credits, processing Transactions, generating reports, and ordering Cards.
"Online Intake Portal"
Means the optional web-based interface within the CardONE System through which Riders may submit enrollment or sign-up information directly to Licensee's program.
"Rider Sign-In Portal"
Means the optional web-based interface within the CardONE System through which Riders may authenticate, view their ride history, check balances, and request trip bookings.
"Rider Data"
Means all personally identifiable information and paratransit program data pertaining to Riders that is collected, submitted, or generated through Licensee's use of the CardONE System.

§ 3Services & Deliverables

3.1 Core Services. Subject to this Agreement and payment of fees, CabConnect shall provide Licensee with the following services:

(a) CardONE Platform Access

Access to the Admin Portal for Authorized Users to manage Rider accounts, load and monitor ride credits, process Transactions, generate usage reports, and coordinate Card fulfillment.

(b) Card Production & Fulfillment

Production and fulfillment of physical Cards upon Licensee's request, subject to a minimum order quantity of 500 Cards per order. Cards will be produced and shipped to Licensee's designated address within a mutually agreed lead time, generally ten (10) to fifteen (15) business days from order confirmation.

(c) Transaction Processing

Real-time processing of Transactions initiated by Riders using issued Cards, including authorization, settlement, and balance management within the CardONE System.

3.2 Optional Add-On Services. Upon written request and payment of applicable additional fees, CabConnect will enable the following optional modules:

(a) Online Intake / Sign-Up Portal

A branded web portal through which prospective Riders may submit enrollment applications to Licensee's paratransit program electronically.

(b) Rider Sign-In Portal

A secure, authenticated portal through which enrolled Riders may view their Card balance, Transaction history, and request trip bookings.

3.3 Support. CabConnect will provide Licensee with reasonable technical support via email and telephone during CabConnect's standard business hours (Monday–Friday, 9:00 AM–5:00 PM Pacific Time), excluding federal and California state holidays.
