The Opportunity
A $410M market with no clinical leader.
The wellness shots sub-category is a $410M market growing at 7.6% annually — with Suja ($327M), So Good So You ($100M+), and every major brand making inflammation and immunity claims on zero clinical evidence. The top two brands control 70%+ of market share. Both are PE-backed, retail-built, and science-free. Paro changes that.
The Differentiation
The world's only clinical-data-backed ginger shot.
Paro is the only wellness shot brand built on Evithé Biotechnology's patented, Phase 1-validated ginger extract — the only ginger extract in the world with human clinical data.

50 participants. 7 validated inflammatory biomarkers. 0 adverse events. 3 patents.

Evithé's proprietary hormesis process stresses ginger to force release of defense compounds, producing an extract standardized to ~20% gingerols/shogaols — delivering 100mg active phenolics per shot, 30–50× more bioavailable than commodity alternatives. This is not a marketing story. It is a scientific moat.
