Executive Summary
The Opportunity

Domo Cafe is the first official Domo-kun cafe in the United States — a licensed, NHK-sanctioned destination with built-in global brand recognition and a passionate fanbase. The marketing opportunity is extraordinary: earned media is abundant, the audience is pre-qualified, and the Grand Opening on June 13, 2026 is a one-time moment that demands flawless execution.

The Domo Cafe Marketing Portal centralizes every marketing operation — content planning, campaign management, influencer partnerships, email automation, loyalty triggers, event coordination, subscription box marketing, and press — in a single system purpose-built for this brand.

📍
Local
Buena Park/SoCal community. Families, anime fans, foodies. Drive repeat visits and word-of-mouth.
✈️
Destination
Disney-area tourists, Japanese culture visitors. Position Domo Cafe as a must-visit SoCal stop.
🌐
National
Domo fans everywhere via subscription box, digital drops, social content, and online merch.
Grand Opening Embargo

Interior photos and full website are embargoed until June 13, 2026 at 10:00 AM. The Marketing Portal must enforce this embargo in the content calendar — no interior content may be scheduled or published before that moment. Media gets the first reveal.

02
Problem Statement
Where We Are Today

With Grand Opening approaching, Domo Cafe has no centralized marketing system. Content is created and posted ad hoc. There is no campaign calendar, no approval workflow, no influencer database, and no analytics view. For a brand of this significance, that gap is a risk.

No Content Calendar
Posts go live without coordination across channels. No visibility into what's going out when, or who approved it.
NHK Compliance Gap
No system to flag content using Domo-kun assets for compliance review before publishing. Any unauthorized use risks the license.
No Campaign ROI
No way to measure which campaigns drive visits, Chomps redemptions, or subscription sign-ups. Marketing spend is blind.
Scattered Influencers
No database of creator contacts, rates, past performance, or content review status. Relationships managed via DMs.
⚠️ NHK Compliance Risk

All content using Domo-kun character imagery must align with NHK licensing guidelines. Unauthorized use — including by influencers — could constitute a breach of the Merchandising License Agreement. A compliance gate in the content workflow is non-negotiable.

Any influencer content featuring Domo-kun must be reviewed and approved by the NHK Compliance Reviewer before the creator publishes it. Loyalty Marketing
Chomps as a Marketing Lever

Chomps — Domo Cafe's loyalty currency — is also the primary marketing activation tool. The Marketing Portal controls when and how Chomps multipliers and bonuses are deployed.

✖️
Dynamic Multipliers
Set 2x or 3x Chomps windows for off-peak hours. Example: 2x Chomps every Tuesday 2–5pm. Triggered via Toast occupancy data — auto-activates when occupancy drops below threshold.
🎁
Campaign Bonuses
Tie Chomps bonuses to campaign actions — sign up for email = 50 Chomps. Share a post = 100 Chomps. First visit of the month = 2x.
🏆
Tier-Based Campaigns
Target campaigns by loyalty tier. Domo Legend members get early access to pin drops. Domo Obsessed get exclusive event invites.
📊
Chomps Analytics
Track Chomps issued per campaign, redemption rate, Chomps-to-visit conversion, and revenue generated per Chomps campaign.

Technical Architecture
How It's Built
Frontend
Next.js 14 + TypeScript. React Server Components. Tailwind CSS. Railway hosting.
Database
PostgreSQL on Railway. Prisma ORM. Tables for campaigns, content, assets, creators, contacts, events.
Auth
Clerk or NextAuth. Roles: Operator, Marketing Manager, Content Creator, Social Manager, NHK Reviewer.
Asset Storage
Cloudflare R2 or S3. NHK assets in separate, access-logged bucket.
Email
Resend API. Campaigns + transactional + automations.
Jarvis API
GET /api/jarvis/status + full CRUD. Auth: x-api-key = JARVIS_API_KEY. Required on all builds.
