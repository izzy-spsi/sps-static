Non-Disclosure Agreement

1. Parties

This Non-Disclosure Agreement ("Agreement") is entered into between EZLiving LLC, a limited liability company ("Company"), and the individual identified in the access request form ("Recipient"). 2. Confidential Information

"Confidential Information" means any and all non-public information disclosed by the Company to the Recipient, whether orally, in writing, electronically, or by any other means, including but not limited to: business plans, financial projections, proforma models, investment terms, cap tables, intellectual property, product roadmaps, customer lists, partnership agreements, proprietary technology, and the existence or terms of any offering.

3. Obligations of Recipient

Recipient agrees to: (a) hold all Confidential Information in strict confidence; (b) not disclose Confidential Information to any third party without the prior written consent of the Company; (c) use Confidential Information solely for the purpose of evaluating a potential investment in the Company; and (d) protect Confidential Information with at least the same degree of care used to protect its own confidential information, but in no event less than reasonable care.

4. Exclusions

This Agreement does not apply to information that: (a) is or becomes publicly known through no breach of this Agreement; (b) was rightfully known to Recipient prior to disclosure; (c) is independently developed by Recipient without use of Confidential Information; or (d) is required to be disclosed by applicable law or court order, provided Recipient gives Company prompt written notice and cooperates with Company's efforts to seek a protective order.

5. Non-Circumvention

Recipient agrees not to circumvent, avoid, bypass, or obviate the Company's interests, directly or indirectly, in any manner whatsoever, with respect to any transaction, opportunity, relationship, or information introduced or disclosed by the Company to Recipient under this Agreement.

6. Return of Information

Upon request by the Company, Recipient shall promptly return or destroy all Confidential Information and any copies thereof, and certify in writing that such return or destruction has been completed.

7. Term

This Agreement shall remain in effect for a period of three (3) years from the date of electronic acceptance. Obligations with respect to trade secrets shall survive indefinitely.

8. Governing Law

This Agreement shall be governed by the laws of the State of Nevada, without regard to its conflict of law provisions. Any disputes shall be resolved exclusively in the state or federal courts located in Clark County, Nevada.
