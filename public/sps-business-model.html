01 — Executive Summary

The operating system for entrepreneurs.

The Problem

Building a company is broken.

Access to attorneys ($500/hr), accountants ($300/hr), consultants, and investors is locked behind money most founders don't have. Most companies fail not because of bad ideas — but because of poor execution infrastructure.

The Solution

AI agents that do the work.

SPS deploys 14 specialized AI agents — Ideation, Formation, Market Intelligence, Legal, Branding, Investor Portal — all hosted and orchestrated by the Founder's personal OpenClaw AI. Available 24/7. Fraction of the cost.

The Opportunity

A $50–75B market. No full-stack AI-native leader.

5.5M new US businesses in 2023 alone. 99% locked out of top accelerators. Traditional accelerator model cracking (Techstars -17% staff, 2024). Harvey AI proves AI legal is $11B+ — but nobody serves founders at scale. SPS owns the white space.
