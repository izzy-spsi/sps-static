OT — Overtime (1.5x)
Hours 8–12 in a single shift or over 40/week. Cost = OT hours × wage × 0.5 premium.
DT — Double Time (2x)
Hours beyond 12 in a single shift or 7th consecutive day. Cost = DT hours × wage × 1.0 premium.
BC — Break Compliance
CA law: 30-min meal break required for shifts >5h. Each violation = 1 hr premium pay at regular rate.
