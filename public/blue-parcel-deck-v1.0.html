The Core Insight

Amazon already sorts and bins packages today. We ask them to load our capsules instead of their bags. Zero change to their existing operation. When the biker scans the capsule off the vessel, their full delivery route is pre-dispatched automatically.

Blue Parcel's cost & operational responsibility = Stages 3–4 only. We own the water transit and last-dock dispatch. Everything else is our partners' existing workflow.
