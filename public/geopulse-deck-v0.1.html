Enter any US address and receive a comprehensive neighborhood analysis in seconds — powered by real public data and AI-generated narratives.

Investment Analysis — 9201 4th Ave
Location Investment Thesis
GeoPulse AI summary for 9201 4th Avenue — Bay Ridge, Brooklyn

✅ Neighborhood Strengths

Zero flood risk — FEMA Zone X confirmed. No flood insurance requirement for buyers.
Walk Score 100 / Transit 100 — Walker's Paradise with R train access. Manhattan commute under 35 min.
9.1M sqft of park space — Dyker Beach Park (210 acres) directly adjacent. Waterfront access, sports fields, bike path.
Below-average crime — NY state violent crime is 4% below national average, property crime 34% below.
74% renters in Brooklyn — Deep pool of renter-to-buyer conversion candidates for condo sales.
Limited new construction — Bay Ridge has constrained supply due to zoning. This project is one of few.
C4-4D zoning approved May 14 — Entitlement risk eliminated. Project can break ground.

⚠️ Considerations

$1,350/sqft assumption — 59% premium over $850 neighborhood median. Requires luxury finish and strong marketing to justify.
Median HH income $60,878 — Existing residents may not be the primary buyer. Target: relocators from Park Slope, Williamsburg, Manhattan.
Investment score 61/100 — Moderate. Appreciation has been steady (4.2%/yr) but not explosive. Best case is absorption speed.
Luxury score 62/100 — Fine dining is limited. The commercial space (14K sqft) presents an opportunity to build the luxury amenity base the neighborhood currently lacks.
School ratings are estimated — GreatSchools integration will replace current NCES-computed ratings with verified scores.
