Sheepshead Bay is a vibrant waterfront community in southern Brooklyn — famous for its marina, fishing boats, and the legendary Emmons Avenue promenade. It's a destination neighborhood with a strong residential demand and a walkable, authentic character that luxury buyers increasingly prize.
1901 sits at the epicenter of the Emmons Avenue strip — with unobstructed water views and direct access to one of New York City's most active recreational waterfronts.
