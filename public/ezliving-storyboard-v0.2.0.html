Platform Layer 3

The EZLiving Exchange

"Just like trading stock — there's a clearing house. You don't need brokers, you don't need anything."

As the EZLiving network grows across buildings and cities, residents gain access to a property exchange platform — direct unit swaps without brokers, agents, or closing friction.

Trade your Brooklyn unit for a Miami EZLiving property — no broker

Clearing house infrastructure manages settlement and title transfer

Pilot: 2 buildings. Scale: unlimited as network grows

Creates massive switching cost — residents stay in the ecosystem

$0
Broker fees on exchange

The clearing house replaces the broker. EZLiving captures the spread as a platform transaction fee.

Network Effect

Every new building that joins the platform increases the value of every existing resident's membership. Classic two-sided marketplace flywheel.
