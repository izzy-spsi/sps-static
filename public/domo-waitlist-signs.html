Host Training Scripts

Host Training Guide — Waitlist Process

Domo Cafe · Internal Use Only

When adding guests to the waitlist

"I'm adding you to our waitlist now. You'll receive text updates throughout your wait. Feel free to enjoy the mall while you wait.

When you receive a notification to return, please come back within 10 minutes and check in with us.

The notification means it's time to return to the café. Once you arrive, we'll place you in our seating queue and seat you as tables become available."

When guests return after receiving notification

"Welcome back! I'll check you in now.

You're in the seating queue and we'll get you seated as soon as the next appropriate table becomes available."

If a guest asks: "Do we get seated immediately?"

"Not necessarily. The text notification lets you know it's time to return to the café. Once you check in, you're placed in the seating queue and we'll seat you as tables become available."

Why This Language Works

  • Prevents guests from expecting an immediate table when they return
  • Encourages them to come back promptly (10-minute window)
  • Gives the team flexibility to keep tables turning without holding them for guests still in the mall
  • Avoids implying that returning guests "cut" ahead of physically waiting guests
