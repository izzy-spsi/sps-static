🗺️Run of Show — Tour Route
▾
1 🚪 Arrival & Welcome — Zone 1 ▾

Welcome guests at the entrance and begin the tour from Zone 1.

  • Show where the long line will curve around the entrance area.
  • Share the story of when we opened Gudetama Cafe here — the energy, the lines, what that moment felt like.
💡 Set the tone early. This is a moment of excitement — channel the Gudetama opening energy.
2 🎨 Zone 2 — Mural & Surroundings ▾
  • Point out and showcase the mural and the sign.
  • Show guests Soak City from this vantage point.
  • Highlight the free parking — it's a big deal for families.
3 🐰 Meet the Characters ▾

Introduce guests to the world of Domo. Use these character backgrounds to bring the story to life.

🐰 Mr. Usaji (the Rabbit)
Domo's cave companion. A wise old rabbit who has lived in the cave for decades. He loves watching television and drinking astringent green tea. Mr. Usaji is not into "new" materials and does not own a telephone.

Fashion: Focuses on materials over shapes.
Favorite food: Carrots.
Least favorite food: "Something that is meaningless."

His Japanese name "Usajii" is a portmanteau of usagi (rabbit) and jii (grandpa/old man).
🦦 Tashanna (the Weasel)
17 years old. Aspires to be a fashion stylist or model in Tokyo. Always using technology — televisions, mobile phones, and cameras.

Accent (English): "Weaselly" — ends sentences with "y"s.
Japanese version: Ends sentences with "chi".
Fun fact: Has not had a boyfriend in ten years and is seeking a platonic one. Passionate about auctions (but gives up by the end).
Favorite food: Apricot and mint tarts.
Least favorite: Sea urchin.

Her name originates from the Japanese word for "multichannel" in digital broadcasting.
🦇 Maya & Mario (the Bats)
The two iconic bats of the Domo universe. Share what makes them part of the cave family.
4 🎢 Zone 4 — Knotts View & Development ▾
  • As you walk to Zone 4, point out the Knotts Berry Farm view.
  • Explain the construction happening nearby.
  • Highlight the 1,300 new apartments being built — this means thousands of new neighbors and potential regulars for Domo Cafe.
💡 Frame the apartment development as a growth opportunity — we're opening at exactly the right moment for this neighborhood.
5 🚪 Zones 5, 6 & 7 — Exit & Future Patio ▾
  • Walk through Zones 5, 6, and 7.
  • Show guests this is the exit area.
  • Point out the space designated for a future patio — share the vision for outdoor seating and expansion.
6 👨‍🍳 Back to Entrance — Chef's Hat ▾

Walk back to the entrance and point out the chef's hat feature. Let guests appreciate the design detail and theming at the front of the cafe.

7 ☀️ Morning Opening Demonstration ▾

Show guests how we open the cafe each morning. Walk them through the opening ritual — what the team does, how the space comes to life, and what the first moments of a Domo Cafe day look like.

💡 This is a behind-the-scenes moment — guests love seeing the "backstage" of a cafe they love.
